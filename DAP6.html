Captain Book

Captain Book, a bookstore chain, after opening quite few physical stores made the decision to go online which was a success.

To further develop the company, an analyze of the company sales was requested to target new areas of growth.

Protocol:

  1. Cleaning of the data by treating missing values and outliers.
  2. Preliminary analysis the data with:
    1. Indicators of central tendency and dispersion.
    2. A concentration analysis, using a Lorenz curve and a Gini coefficient.
    3. Bivariate analyses.
  3. Correlation analysis
    1. Is there a correlation between gender and categories of products purchased?
    2. Is there a correlation between age and:
      1. The total amount of purchases?
      2. The purchase frequency (the number of purchases per month for example)?
      3. The average basket size (in number of items)?
      4. Categories of purchased products?

Data used:

Datasets extracted from company database:
  • Sales (called "Transactions").
  • List of customers.
  • List of products.
